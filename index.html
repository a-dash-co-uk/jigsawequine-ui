About Jigsaw Equine

Debbie at Jigsaw Equine has been coaching horse riders of all levels since 2006 working with both amateurs and professionals across multiple equine disciplines.

Having delivered numerous talks for Riding Clubs and Riding Club AGMs with an inspiring delivery style, her talks embrace very practical exercises with a keen sense of humour. This winning combination shines a light on the key elements of psychology that riders grapple with on a daily basis.

Jigsaw Equine Services

Debbie's Jigsaw Equine services include building rider confidence, managing rider nerves and preparing for competitions.

Debbie has an affinity for helping riders get better results from their riding lessons so that success at home can be repeated when in the competition arena.

Trainers also can be supported in learning how to better communicate with riders of all levels.
